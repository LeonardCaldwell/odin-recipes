Buttered Noodles

Home

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Butter
  • Parmesan
  • Salt and Pepper

Method

  1. Boil the Fettucine Pasta until it's tender.
  2. Drain the pasta in a colander then return it to the pot.
  3. Mix butter, cheese, salt, and pepper into the cooked noodles until all ingredients are evenly coated.
